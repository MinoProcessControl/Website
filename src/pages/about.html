About MINO

Our parent company, MINO S.p.A is a privately owned Italian company who designs and builds rolling mills. Since its foundation in 1840 MINO has built on its reputation as one of the oldest established and most well known providers of rolling mills. MINO rolling mills can be traced as far back as the silver trade in Italy during the late 19th century.

Since those early years of the industrial revolution to the present MINO has been at the forefront of rolling mill technology - rolling and processing was, and remains, the beating heart of our business. Today as always MINO provides its clients with the very latest technology, engineering design and innovation for production of ferrous and non-ferrous metals.
