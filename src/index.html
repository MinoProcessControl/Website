About Us

MINO Process Control

MINO Process Control designs, builds and supplies state-of-the-art process control systems for rolling mills with our primary product focused on the control of strip thickness and flatness. We were founded in 2010 as an addition to MINO group - our skill and know-how complementing the existing rolling mill technology within MINO.

We will be pleased to help make your rolling mill deliver its full potential in quality and production.
